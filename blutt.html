My Last Name

My last name is Blutt. It is pronounced Bloot or Blutte. It has German, Slovenian, and Luxembourg origins. In Luxembourgish, it translates to blood. My pronunciation is Bloot because the "TT" (double consonant) makes the "U" (vowel) have a hard sound.
